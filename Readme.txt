How to play :
Kill every zombie that will come to get you at every waves and survive the longest possible.

1 - 4 players can play the game (we using localhost for this one right now).

To start a server, write your name, click ok, then click on (Host), more players can join you.

Once every players are ready by clicking (Ready), you click (Start) and you are teleported into our map where you start the game.

If all players die during a wave, the game end and you go back to the menu.




Control : 
W, A, S, D : Move controls

Scroll down/up : Switch weapons

Right click : Aim

Left click : Shoot

R : Reload

Moving your mouse : Turn the camera
